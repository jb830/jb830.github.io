About

Hi, I’m Jo. I am currently working towards a bachelor’s degree in web development at Bellevue University with a focus on the MEAN stack. I am passionate about front-end design but also love to code and understand how things work holistically to make beautiful user-friendly applications come together. After I had my son, I stopped working so I could stay home with him and that’s when my brother introduced me to HTML and CSS. I had so much fun with it, I spent almost a year teaching myself using free online sources and making small projects before deciding to go back to school to study web design and programming. I did an internship nearly a year into my schooling and that sealed the deal. I simply love web design and development and the opportunities it offers for continual learning and self-improvement.
